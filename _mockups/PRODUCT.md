# Hosamine — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Hosamine SARL is a Douala-based environmental services company. Two surfaces:
1. **Public website** (hosamine.net) — marketing and lead generation for B2B institutional clients
2. **Internal tools** — CRM workflow and social media management for operations team

## Register
- Website: **brand** (marketing, lead gen, credibility)
- Internal tools: **product** (app UI, dashboards, workflows)

## Users

### Website visitors
- Procurement and QHSE managers at oil & gas, banking, healthcare, logistics firms in Cameroon
- Decision-makers evaluating pest control / fumigation / sanitation contractors
- Anglophones (CBCH, DHL, Schlumberger) and Francophones
- Goal: verify credentials, understand service scope, initiate contact

### Internal tool users
- Hosamine commercial team (6–10 people, Douala)
- Social media manager
- Managers reviewing pipeline and billing
- Not highly technical; French-speaking primarily

## Brand

### Identity
- **Name:** Hosamine SARL
- **Tagline:** "Au service d'une vie saine" / "At the service of a healthy life"
- **Positioning:** Expert en Solutions Environnementales et Agricoles
- **Certifications:** MINADER + MINSANTE authorized, Port Autonome de Douala partner
- **Founded:** ~2020, 5 years experience as of 2025
- **Size:** 17 staff

### Brand personality
Authoritative, precise, trustworthy, professional. Not corporate-cold — they serve hospitals and communities. Not casual — they serve Schlumberger and DHL. The tone is confident expertise.

### Colors
- Primary: deep forest green (authentic to environmental sector identity — not a cliché, earned)
- Accent: warm amber/gold (premium, expertise, contrasts with green)
- Neutrals: very lightly green-tinted whites and greys

### Anti-references
- Generic eco/clipart aesthetics (leaf icons on everything, generic "go green" palette)
- French corporate grey boredom
- SaaS-cream layouts that could belong to any startup
- Identical grid cards with icon + text × 8

## Services
1. **Hygiène Publique** — désinsectisation, dératisation, désinfection, nettoyage professionnel
2. **Traitements Phytosanitaires** — fumigation containers, bâches, bois, cultures, stockage
3. **Assainissement** — vidange fosses septiques, curage caniveaux, eau potable
4. **Formations** — agriculture durable, gestion ressources naturelles (NGO/institutional clients)

## Clients (reference)
NHPC, Schlumberger, NOVIA Industries, SAICAM, DHL, UBA, Cameroon Baptist Church Hospital, Security Boat Services, CCIMA, Padre Pio, Glym Africa, CED

## Languages
Bilingual FR/EN with toggle. French is primary. English for international/anglophone clients.

## Tone
- Headlines: bold declarative ("Expertise certifiée. Résultats garantis.")
- Body: precise, service-focused, no fluff
- English: professional without over-formality
